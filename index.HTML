What is ThermoBuddy?

ThermoBuddy is a joyful desk friend that changes color with temperature — violet-blue under 25°C (77°F) and white above it!

Cold (Violet-Blue)
Warm (White)

Fun Fact: Thermochromic pigments physically shift their structure when heated — that’s why they change color!
